By downloading and installing this add-on, you acknowledge and have reviewed our Privacy Policy ( http://www.facebook.com/policy.php ) and agree to our Terms ( http://www.facebook.com/terms.php ).

While the add-on is installed and the Like feature enabled, it will display the total number of likes for the page you are viewing. In order to provide a personalized experience, Facebook will see some technical information such as the URL of the page you are on, your IP address and the date and time you visited the site. All information collected before you log into Facebook is anonymous or aggregated and will not identify you.
